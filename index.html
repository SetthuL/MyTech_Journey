🚀 About Me

👩‍🚀 From Sci-Fi Enthusiast to Self-Taught Technologist

Ever since childhood, I’ve been mesmerized by the futuristic worlds of sci-fi movies — those glowing screens, intelligent systems, and data-driven heroes. 💡 I saw myself among them — a digital problem solver who could shape the future with code.

My first dream? ✈️ To soar the skies as a pilot. But when that path dimmed due to finances and lack of opportunity, I looked to another sky — the digital one. Technology became my cockpit — a place where skills, curiosity, and courage mattered more than credentials.

💻 From Education Admin to Tech Innovator

My professional journey took off at Sitoromo Primary School, where I worked as an Education Assistant in ICT. What began as supporting teachers quickly turned into leading change — integrating tech in classrooms, optimizing LMS platforms, and ensuring ICT tools ran smoothly for both learners and educators.

From data management to system audits, I became the go-to person for anything digital. This experience transformed me — showing how a few lines of code could solve real problems and boost efficiency in ways I never imagined.

🧠 Becoming a Self-Taught Programmer

When formal studies weren’t possible, I decided to write my own syllabus. I immersed myself in online learning, open-source projects, and late-night debugging sessions — turning curiosity into capability, and capability into confidence. ⚡
